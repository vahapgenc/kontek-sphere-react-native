# Kontek — Design System

Kontek is a calm, professional payroll & people platform for small and mid-sized
companies. It brings three modules — **Lön** (payroll), **Time** (time & attendance)
and **Sphere** (HR & employee data) — together behind one quiet, confident surface.

The feel we're after: software-grade and restrained. Your payroll is handled —
here is the one thing that needs you.

---

## How to use this system

1. **Start from the tokens.** Use the colors, type, spacing and radii defined here
   rather than inventing new ones. They're tuned to work together.
2. **Lean on the components.** Buttons, inputs, cards, badges and navigation are
   already designed — reuse them before building something new.
3. **Keep the layout.** A dark green sidebar against a soft off-white canvas, with
   content on white cards, is the system's signature shape.
4. **Match the voice.** Calm, warm, Swedish, second person. (See *Voice* below.)
5. **One primary action per screen.** Let whitespace carry the calm.

---

## Colors

| Role | Color |
|---|---|
| Sidebar & top bar | Dark forest green |
| Page background | Soft, warm off-white |
| Cards, tables, forms, modals | White |
| Primary button | Deep teal-green |
| Approve / save button | Accent green with dark text |
| Destructive actions only | Red |
| Body text | Near-black |

**Rules of thumb**
- Most surfaces are a quiet, green-tinted neutral.
- Accent green marks positive actions, active states, icons and progress.
- Red is *only* for destructive or irreversible actions — never for save or approve.
- No decorative gradients. Backgrounds stay flat, warm and matte.

---

## Type

One typeface throughout — **Open Sans**. Hierarchy comes from size and weight, not a
second font.

- Headings and greetings run large and bold.
- Body and interface text sit smaller and lighter.
- Sentence case everywhere. Eyebrow labels are UPPERCASE with wide spacing.
- Never Title Case headings.

---

## Voice

The product speaks Swedish, warm but professional, and talks to the user directly
("du", "dig").

- **Reassure first, then give specifics.** *"Lönekörningen är klar för granskning ·
  32 anställda · inga avvikelser."*
- **Personal, never corporate.** *"God morgon, Anna."*
- **Verbs on buttons** — an action you take, not a feature name (*Granska & godkänn*,
  *Ny körning*, *Visa detaljer*).
- **No emoji. No hype.** Numbers are concrete.

---

## Look & feel

- **Two zones.** Dark green sidebar, light canvas, white cards.
- **Gentle corners.** Soft, consistent radii — never sharp, never overly round.
- **Hairline borders + soft shadows** do the separating. Cards lift slightly on hover.
- **Restrained motion.** Quick, smooth, no bounce or spin. Respect reduced-motion.
- **Comfortable density.** Roomy, not cramped — whitespace signals calm.

---

## Icons & logo

- **Icons:** clean line icons, single color, drawn from the curated sets in this system.
  On light surfaces they take the brand green; never multicolor, never emoji.
- **Logo:** use the supplied Kontek wordmark — green on light, white on dark backgrounds.
  Keep clear space around it and don't go below the minimum size.
