Dialog

A centred modal for decisions that must happen before anything else — confirming an irreversible action or surfacing a blocking error. An optional icon sets the tone (green for confirm, red for destructive). Actions stack full-width with the primary on top; short either/or choices may sit side by side. Destructive confirmations use the danger button.

Riktlinjer

Dos & don'ts

Do
  • Reserve the dialog for irreversible or high-stakes confirmations.
  • Use the --danger button + danger icon for destructive confirms.
  • Stack actions full-width with the primary on top.
Don't
  • Pop a dialog to confirm a reversible action — use optimistic undo.
  • Colour a destructive confirm green, or a safe one red.
  • Offer more than two choices in a confirmation dialog.
