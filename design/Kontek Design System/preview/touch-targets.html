Allt interaktivt är minst 48px — samma säkra minimum på iOS och Android. Utöka träffytan med padding, inte en större ikon.

One safe minimum for both platforms

iOS asks for ≥ 44pt and Android Material for ≥ 48dp — we standardise on 48 so nothing is ever borderline. A control may look smaller, but its hit area is padded out to 48; comfortable spacing between targets prevents mis-taps.

Riktlinjer

Dos & don'ts

Do
  • Keep every interactive element at least 48px (--tap-min) on iOS and Android.
  • Expand a small glyph's hit area with padding, not a bigger icon.
  • Space adjacent targets so their hit areas never overlap.
Don't
  • Ship a sub-48px tap target, even for a tiny icon.
  • Crowd two targets so a thumb hits both.
  • Rely on the visible art size — size the hit area.
