App bar

The sticky top chrome of every screen. Three forms share one component: large-title (the standard Översikt / Lön / Time header — eyebrow + 28px title), compact (centred title with leading + trailing actions, for deeper screens) and a dark variant for forest-toned surfaces. Translucent with a blur so content scrolls under it. Action targets are a full 48px.

Riktlinjer

Dos & don'ts

Do
  • Use the large-title bar on root tabs, the compact back-chevron bar on pushed screens.
  • Put the notifications bell in the right slot with a count badge.
  • Keep it transparent at rest and frost it in on scroll.
Don't
  • Give the resting bar a solid fill or a divider.
  • Crowd more than two actions into the bar.
  • Title Case the screen title.
