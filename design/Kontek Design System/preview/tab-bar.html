Bottom tab bar

The primary mobile navigation — four core areas plus a raised center action, permanently in thumb reach. It replaces side navigation (sidonavigering) on phones. Selection is carried by color alone — no pill, underline, or fill on the active tab. The center “+” is not a tab: it triggers the Register action sheet and never shows a selected state. Identical on iPhone and Android; the bar honours the home-indicator safe area.

Riktlinjer

Dos & don'ts

Do
  • Carry selection by colour alone — active = --signature.
  • Swap slots 2 & 4 by role (employee: Pay+Calendar; manager: Me+Employees).
  • Put count badges on the icon as a green circle with a white number.
Don't
  • Add a pill, underline or fill to mark the active tab.
  • Exceed five slots or treat the centre + as a tab.
  • Put a count on the label instead of the icon.
